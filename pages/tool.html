Quick Guide:

Here is a quick guide for those of you who don't quite know what's going on here.

First, enter your slayer level, combat level, and block slots, then pick the masters that you want to test.
You don't have to select them all - obviously if you're high combat, you don't want to test Turael.

If you pick Nieve, please enter whether or not you've done western provinces elite.

For each task, enter whether or not it's possible, and if it is, put in a preference value.
NOTE THAT YOUR BANS DO NOT COUNT TOWARDS WHETHER OR NOT A TASK IS POSSIBLE!!! The tool will determine your best set of bans. If you have a task that you ban because you hate it, just turn down the preference for it.

If you wish, you can turn on the "verbose input" option, to put in values on your own personal scale. Any scale is fine, as long as larger numbers mean more preferred tasks. You can use negative numbers and decimals.

If you pick Krystilia, you can substitute in the preference values you've already entered for the wilderness versions, but beware that some tasks are significantly different (e.g., no baby black dragons in the wilderness).

Enjoy! If you find anything weird, email me at wewhrothik@gmail.com
